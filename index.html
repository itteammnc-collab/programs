Program Overview

Operating under the vision of the Mon National Education Committee (MNEC), this program is engineered directly for community service. It addresses the critical administrative needs of local civil society organizations, self-help committees, and decentralized education infrastructure.

By blending core management essentials with regional conflict resolution strategies, the curriculum ensures that students remain deeply rooted in Mon cultural contexts while acquiring the practical, modern administrative skills necessary to cooperate with international development partners.

Why Study at Mon National College?

Career Prospects

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, MNEC offers partial and full financial support for students demonstrating high community commitment and financial need.

Can I apply if I didn't graduate from a Mon National School?

Yes. While MNC prioritizes Mon National School graduates, students with equivalent government or private high school degrees who show strong language skills and community dedication are welcome to apply.

Does the College provide accommodation?

MNC provides basic dormitory facilities for students traveling from distant townships or rural regions on a priority basis.
